Delhi

Delhi is a city that needs no introduction. It is the capital of India and known as the "City of rallies". It is famous for its art, culture, history, fashion, and gastronomy. There is so much to see and do in Delhi that one trip is not enough to experience it all.

  1. India Gate
  2. Laal Kila
  3. Lotus Temple
  4. Askhar Dham
  5. Jantar Mantar
  • Croissants: A flaky and buttery pastry that is a must-try in Paris.
  • Macarons: A colorful and delicate French dessert that comes in a variety of flavors.
  • Escargots: A French delicacy of snails cooked in garlic butter and served in their shells.
  • Crème Brûlée: A classic French dessert consisting of a creamy custard topped with caramelized sugar.
  • Baguette: A long and crispy French bread that is a staple in every Parisian household.
